Piarom Dates History

According to the archaeological evidences, the palm planting was prevalent in Iran near the former Shoosh at the time of Islamic civilization about 6000 years. Piarom is one of dates that grows in Hajiabad city of Hormozgan province in southern Iran (one of the 31 provinces of Iran).
